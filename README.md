## Sandeep Makasare Portfolio

## Overview
Welcome to Sandeep Makasare's portfolio website! This website showcases the culinary expertise and creative flair of Sandeep Makasare, a passionate chef with a penchant for creating delectable dishes. Through this website, visitors can explore Sandeep's culinary journey, discover his signature recipes, and learn more about his professional background and achievements.

## Features
- Home: A warm welcome and brief introduction to Sandeep Makasare.
- About: Detailed information about Sandeep's culinary background, experience, and philosophy.
- Recipes: A curated collection of Sandeep's signature recipes, accompanied by mouthwatering images and detailed instructions.
- Gallery: Stunning visuals of Sandeep's culinary creations, showcasing his artistic presentation and attention to detail.
- Contact: Contact information for Sandeep Makasare, including email and social media links, allowing visitors to connect with him directly.

## Technologies Used
HTML: Used for structuring the content of the website.
CSS: Used for styling and enhancing the visual appeal of the website.

## How to Use
- Clone the repository to your local machine.
- Open the index.html file in your preferred web browser.
- Explore Sandeep Makasare's portfolio, navigate through the different sections, and enjoy the culinary journey!
